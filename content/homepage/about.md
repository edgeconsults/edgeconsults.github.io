Dr. Edwin Solares is a researcher, Principal Investigator, and Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering and the Halicioglu Data Science Institute at UC San Diego, where he teaches upper-division and graduate-level courses in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Big Data Analytics. He has authored 14+ peer-reviewed publications in computational biology and methods development, secured and led federal grants as Principal Investigator, built and administered HPC clusters, and generated the first intellectual property for a previous industry employer. Founder of ESB AI Lab, a California 501(c)(3) nonprofit advancing AI for science. Ph.D. in Ecology & Evolutionary Biology, B.S. in Computer Science.
