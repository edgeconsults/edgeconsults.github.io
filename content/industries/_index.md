## Agriculture & Crop Genomics

Cultivar authentication, population genetics for breeding programs, genomic selection, and AI-directed breeding. We build and manage the computational infrastructure that modern crop research demands, from HPC clusters to cloud-based analysis pipelines.

We've worked with avocado, maize, and grapevine, and our methods transfer to any crop.

**What we provide:**
- Cultivar authentication (SNP-based PCR diagnostics)
- Population genetics for breeding programs
- Genomic selection and AI-directed breeding
- Cloud and HPC infrastructure for agricultural research
- Grant support for USDA, FFAR, and commodity board programs

**Relevant services:** [Bioinformatics & Genomics](/services/bioinformatics/), [AI & Machine Learning](/services/ai-ml/), [Grant Writing](/services/grant-writing/), [Data Engineering](/services/data-engineering/)

**Selected projects:** [Avocado Domestication & Population Genomics](/work/avocado-genomics/), [Grapevine Structural Variation](/work/grapevine-structural-variants/)
