EdgeConsults offers workshops for researchers, students, and industry teams. Each workshop combines lecture content with hands-on exercises so participants leave with working code and practical skills.

---

## Applied Reinforcement Learning with Minecraft

Learn MDP fundamentals, value/policy iteration, and agent design using Minecraft as a training environment. Participants build and train their own agents through progressively challenging tasks.

**Suitable for:** Graduate students, early-career researchers, industry ML engineers

**Format:** Half-day hands-on workshop

---

## Bioinformatics Pipeline Development

Build reproducible NGS analysis pipelines from raw reads to variant calls. Covers quality control, alignment, variant calling, and annotation using containerized workflows.

**Suitable for:** Wet-lab researchers, new bioinformaticians

**Format:** Full-day workshop

---

## AI for Agriculture & Aquaculture

Computer vision, genomic prediction, and edge deployment for agricultural applications. Case studies drawn from active USDA-funded research in sturgeon and abalone aquaculture.

**Suitable for:** Ag-tech companies, extension researchers, breeding programs

**Format:** Half-day seminar + hands-on

---

## Cloud Infrastructure for Research Labs

AWS/GCP setup, SLURM cluster administration, Docker containerization, and job scheduling. Designed for teams setting up or scaling their own compute infrastructure.

**Suitable for:** Lab managers, research computing staff, PIs setting up new labs

**Format:** Full-day hands-on
