A great idea dies without a great proposal. We write and support grant applications for NSF, USDA, NIH, and SBIR/STTR programs, with the technical credibility of a PI who has won them.

### What We Do

- Full proposal writing and editing (NSF, USDA NIFA, NIH, SBIR/STTR, FFAR)
- Budget development and justification
- NSF biographical sketches and current & pending support documents
- Letters of commitment and collaboration agreements
- Specific aims and research strategy development
- Broader impacts and data management plans
- Proposal review and red-teaming before submission

**Track record:** Active USDA NIFA WRAC grant as PI. NSF ACCESS compute allocations. Multiple federal fellowships (NSF GRFP, NSF-LSAMP, UC President's Pre-Professoriate, UC President's Postdoctoral).

### Deliverables

- Complete proposal draft ready for PI review and submission
- Budget spreadsheet with detailed justification narrative
- NSF biosketch, current & pending support, and collaboration plan documents
- Letters of commitment for collaborative proposals
- Red-team review summary with scoring rubric and revision recommendations
