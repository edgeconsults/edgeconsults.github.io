## From Cloud to Field

Many research and industry applications need inference at the point of data collection — on a fish farm, in an ecological monitoring station, or at a processing facility. EdgeConsults builds the full stack from model training in the cloud to deployment on embedded hardware in the field.

### What We Do

**Edge AI Deployment** — Porting trained models to NVIDIA Jetson platforms (Orin, Orin Nano) for real-time inference without cloud connectivity. Optimizing models for edge constraints — memory, power, latency.

**Hardware Prototyping** — Rapid prototyping of enclosures, mounting systems, and custom components using 3D printing (including belt-system continuous printing) and CNC machining (Bosch-spindle CNC and Carvera desktop CNC).

**Data Collection Systems** — Designing and deploying sensor platforms with long-range communication for remote field sites. Camera systems, environmental sensors, and telemetry integrated with edge compute.

**Over-the-Air Updates** — Building OTA update pipelines between cloud servers and edge devices so that models can be iteratively improved as new training data is collected.

### Equipment & Infrastructure

EdgeConsults owns and operates GPUs, servers, GCP cloud compute, four NVIDIA Jetson Orins, one Jetson Orin Nano, 3D printers (including a belt-system printer for continuous production), a large custom CNC with Bosch spindle, a Carvera desktop CNC, and various embedded systems peripherals. All equipment is available for client engagements.
