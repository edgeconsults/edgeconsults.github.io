## Production-Grade ML for Research

EdgeConsults develops and deploys machine learning models purpose-built for biological and ecological applications. We work across the full ML lifecycle — from data engineering and feature extraction through model training, validation, and deployment.

### What We Do

**Computer Vision** — Classification and detection models for biological imaging, including ultrasound-based tissue classification and morphological analysis. Our published work on sex determination in Red Abalone (Haliotis rufescens) using deep learning demonstrates real-world deployment of these methods.

**Genotype-to-Phenotype Prediction** — Dimensional reduction, transformer architectures, and gradient-boosted-tree approaches for genome-wide phenotype prediction from SNP genotype data.

**Neural Network Architecture Design** — Custom deep learning architectures including convolutional networks, transformers, and hybrid approaches tailored to the structure of biological data.

**Model Optimization & Deployment** — Training on cloud infrastructure (GCP) and deploying to edge devices (NVIDIA Jetson) for inference at the point of data collection.

### Relevant Experience

Lead PI on the Western Regional Aquaculture Center grant for AI-assisted sex determination in sturgeon — a $465K project developing computer vision models deployed on edge hardware. Lecturer in Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Big Data Analytics at UC San Diego's Department of Computer Science and Engineering and Halıcıoğlu Data Science Institute.
