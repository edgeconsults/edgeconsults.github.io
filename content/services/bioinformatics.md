## End-to-End Genomic Analysis

EdgeConsults provides comprehensive bioinformatics consulting spanning the full arc from raw sequencing data to biological insight. Our expertise covers both established and cutting-edge methodologies, informed by years of independent research at UC San Diego and UC Davis.

### What We Do

**Genome Assembly & Scaffolding** — De novo assembly using long-read sequencing technologies with optimization approaches for removing secondary haplotigs and improving assembly quality. Our published tool HapSolo demonstrates this capability at scale.

**Variant Calling & Structural Variant Detection** — SNP genotyping, indel calling, and detection of large-scale structural variants including inversions, duplications, and translocations across plant and animal genomes.

**Population Genetics** — Analysis of population structure, demographic history, domestication signals, and genetic diversity in crop and aquaculture species. Published work in avocado, grapevine, and maize genomics.

**Pipeline Development** — Custom bioinformatics pipelines optimized for your data, from quality control through final analysis. Designed for reproducibility and scalability on HPC and cloud platforms.

### Relevant Experience

Our bioinformatics work spans crop genomics (avocado, maize, grapevine), conservation biology (Drosophila melanogaster reference genome), and disease vector genomics (Plasmodium vivax). Each engagement draws on 14+ peer-reviewed publications and deep familiarity with federal research standards.
