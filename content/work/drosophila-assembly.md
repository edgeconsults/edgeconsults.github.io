## Democratizing Genome Assembly

**Published:** G3: Genes|Genomes|Genetics, 2018
**Role:** Co-First Author

### Overview

We developed a rapid, low-cost approach to assemble the Drosophila melanogaster reference genome using low-coverage, long-read sequencing. This methodology reduces costs by approximately 90% while maintaining high assembly quality, making reference-grade genome assembly accessible to labs with limited resources.

### Impact

- 90% cost reduction compared to traditional methods
- Methodology adopted by research groups globally
- Foundational for subsequent comparative and population genomics studies
- Demonstrated feasibility of high-quality assemblies with minimal sequencing coverage
